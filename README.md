# 📊 Sales Dashboard Analysis

## 🔍 Problem Statement
Analyze a 7-day book sales dataset to identify revenue gaps, customer behavior, and business risks.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Analysis)
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

## 📈 Key Insights
- Revenue ≠ Volume: Power BI book generated highest revenue despite lower sales
- ₹15K+ revenue lost due to payment failures
- Heavy dependency on discount coupons (FRIDAY80, WINTER70)
- Regional differences in buying behavior (Maharashtra vs Bihar)
- Sudden demand spikes without system readiness

## ⚠️ Business Problems Identified
- High payment failure rate (14.8%)
- Over-reliance on discounts
- Lack of checkout recovery system
- No region-specific marketing strategy

## 💡 Recommendations
- Fix payment gateway issues to recover lost revenue
- Reduce discount dependency and improve pricing strategy
- Implement retry & alert system for failed payments
- Use geo-targeted campaigns
- Prepare systems for demand spikes

## 📁 Project Files
- `sales-data.csv` → Dataset
- `sales-dashboard.pbix` → Power BI Dashboard

## 🚀 Outcome
Converted raw sales data into actionable insights that highlight revenue leakage, operational risks, and growth opportunities.
